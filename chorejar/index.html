How to use ChoreJar

  1. Add each kid from the Today board (no limit on kids).
  2. Add chores, set what each is worth in whole dollars, pick the days and who does them (kids rotate in the order you choose).
  3. A kid taps "✓ Done!" — it lands in the Approve tab, waiting for a parent thumbs-up. Nothing pays out until you approve.
  4. When you approve, the money splits into that kid's Save / Spend / Give jars (change the split in Settings).
  5. Everything stays on YOUR phone. Save a backup file from Settings to move your board to a new phone.

FAQ
