How to use Envelo

  1. Pick your pay cycle (weekly, biweekly, monthly or custom) and create your envelopes — groceries, gas, fun money, whatever your budget needs.
  2. Spend from an envelope in two taps. The remaining balance updates instantly.
  3. When a new cycle starts, open Refill Review: a before → after table you approve line by line. Nothing changes until you say so.
  4. Use goal envelopes for savings targets and scheduled expenses for bills you can't forget.
  5. Settings → Export gives you CSV or a full JSON backup any time. Automatic rotating backups run on their own.

FAQ

Does Envelo connect to my bank?

No, and it never will. You enter what you spend; your bank details never touch the app.

What happens after the 7-day free trial?

Every feature is unlocked during the trial. After 7 days the app asks for one single payment to unlock forever — no subscription, no recurring charges. Your data is never held hostage: CSV export stays available even if you don't buy.

I bought it and reinstalled — how do I get it back?

Settings → Restore purchase. The unlock lives in your App Store / Google account.

Can I change my pay cycle later?

Yes, in Settings. Your history and envelopes are kept.
